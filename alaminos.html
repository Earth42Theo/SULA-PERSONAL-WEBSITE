Why Alaminos is Special to Me

Alaminos, Pangasinan, is famous for the stunning Hundred Islands National Park. Its crystal-clear waters, lush islands, and unique limestone formations make it a dream destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

How to Get There

Alaminos can be reached by bus from Metro Manila, with a travel time of approximately 4-5 hours. Private vehicles also offer a scenic road trip to this coastal paradise.

Other Places to Visit in Alaminos

  • Hundred Islands National Park – Explore over 100 beautiful islands and islets.
  • Lucap Wharf – The gateway to the Hundred Islands and a picturesque spot.
  • Cathedral of St. Joseph – A historic church with a peaceful ambiance.
  • Bolo Beach – A serene beach perfect for relaxation and swimming.
