Why Sagada is Special to Me

Sagada is known for its breathtaking mountain views, rich cultural traditions, and serene atmosphere. I am fascinated by its cool climate, mysterious caves, and the famous hanging coffins that reflect the unique burial practices of the indigenous people.

How to Get There

You can reach Sagada by bus from Manila via Baguio or Banaue. The journey takes approximately 12 hours, offering scenic views of the Cordillera mountains along the way.

Other Places to Visit in Sagada

  • Sumaguing Cave – Explore stunning rock formations inside this famous cave.
  • Echo Valley – Discover the iconic hanging coffins and enjoy the peaceful trail.
  • Bomod-ok Falls – A majestic waterfall perfect for trekking and swimming.
  • Kiltepan Viewpoint – Catch a breathtaking sunrise over a sea of clouds.
