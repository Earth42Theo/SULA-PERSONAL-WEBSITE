Why Baguio is Special to Me

Baguio is known as the "Summer Capital of the Philippines" due to its cool climate and picturesque mountain views. I love its combination of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and the relaxing atmosphere that offers a refreshing escape from city life.

How to Get There

Baguio is easily accessible by bus from Metro Manila, with a travel time of approximately 4-6 hours via major highways like NLEX and TPLEX.

Other Places to Visit in Baguio

  • Burnham Park – A central spot for boating, biking, and picnicking.
  • Camp John Hay – A historic site with scenic trails and leisure spots.
  • Mines View Park – Known for its stunning mountain views and souvenir shops.
  • The Mansion – The official summer residence of the Philippine President.
