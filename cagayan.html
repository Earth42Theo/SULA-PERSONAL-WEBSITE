Why Cagayan is Special to Me

Cagayan is a province rich in natural beauty and cultural heritage. Its stunning beaches, historical sites, and serene landscapes make it a perfect escape. I am drawn to its peaceful environment and the opportunity to explore unspoiled nature.

How to Get There

You can reach Cagayan by plane via Tuguegarao Airport, or by bus from Metro Manila, which takes approximately 12-14 hours of travel.

Other Places to Visit in Cagayan

  • Callao Cave – Famous for its cathedral-like chambers.
  • Palaui Island – A paradise with crystal-clear waters and hiking trails.
  • Baggao Hot Springs – A relaxing retreat in nature.
  • St. Peter’s Cathedral – A historic church in Tuguegarao City.
